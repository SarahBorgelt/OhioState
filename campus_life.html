Student Organizations

We offer a variety of recreational sports, including group fitness, adventure trips, intramural sports, club sports, and much more! We also have over 1,400 student groups.

Giving Back to the Community

Through our Buck-I-SERV program, our students have completed 1.2 million volunteer hours in a variety of locations. Some recent examples of this volunteer work include Habitat for Humanity in Birmingham, Alabama, Bridges to Community in the Dominican Republic, and Hug It Forward in Guatemala.

OSU Sports

Are you a sports fan? If so, you've come to the right place! Ohio State recently won five national championships in one year - not to mention our world-famous marching band!
